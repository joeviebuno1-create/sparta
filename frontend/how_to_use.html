Getting Started in 5 Easy Steps

1

Access SPARTA

Click on the "Talk to SPARTA" button from the main menu. SPARTA is available 24/7 on any device - computer, tablet, or smartphone!

Tip: Bookmark the page for quick access anytime!
2

Type Your Question

Simply type your question in natural language - no special commands needed! SPARTA understands casual conversation.

Example: "Where is Room 301?" or "What are the admission requirements for Engineering?"
3

Get Instant Answers

SPARTHA will respond immediately with accurate, helpful information. If you need more details, just ask follow-up questions!

Follow-up: "Can you tell me more?" or "How do I get there?"
4

Use the 3D Campus Map

Click "Navigate the Campus" to explore BSU Lipa in 3D! Search for locations, get directions, and virtually tour the campus.

Features: Zoom, rotate, and click on buildings to explore different floors!
5

Explore More Features

Check out announcements, browse university history, and discover everything BSU Lipa has to offer through SPARTHA!

Pro Tip: Use specific keywords like "deadline," "schedule," or "contact" for faster results!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SPARTHA free to use?

▼

Yes! SPARTHA is completely free for all BSU Lipa students, faculty, staff, and visitors. No registration or login required!

What kind of questions can I ask?

▼

You can ask about admissions, academic programs, campus locations, faculty contacts, events, schedules, university history, facilities, and much more! If it's related to BSU Lipa, SPARTHA can help!

How accurate is the information?

▼

SPARTHA's information is regularly updated by university administrators to ensure accuracy. However, for official transactions, always verify with the relevant office.

Can SPARTHA help me find my classroom?

▼

Absolutely! Use the "Navigate the Campus" feature to search for any room, building, or facility. The 3D map will show you exactly where it is and how to get there!

What if SPARTHA doesn't understand my question?

▼

Try rephrasing your question or being more specific. If SPARTHA still can't help, you can contact the university directly - SPARTHA can provide you with the right contact information!

Can I use SPARTHA on my phone?

▼

Yes! SPARTHA works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and computers. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size for the best experience.
