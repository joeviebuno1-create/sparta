EMERGENCY EVACUATION

Know your nearest emergency exits

📋 Evacuation Procedures

1. Stay Calm: Don't panic. Follow instructions from faculty or emergency personnel.

2. Use Nearest Exit: Proceed to the closest safe emergency exit. Do not use elevators.

3. Assembly Point: Gather at the designated assembly point (Open Field near Main Gate).

4. Account for Everyone: Faculty will conduct headcount. Report missing persons immediately.

5. Wait for Instructions: Do not re-enter buildings until authorized by emergency personnel.

📞 Emergency Contacts

Campus Security: (043) 756-5555 local 101

Medical Clinic: (043) 756-5555 local 201

Lipa City Emergency: 911

Fire Department: (043) 756-1234

Police Station: (043) 756-4321

⚠️ Safety Reminders

• Know at least two evacuation routes from your current location

• Keep emergency exits clear at all times

• Familiarize yourself with fire alarm sounds

• Assist persons with disabilities during evacuation

• Report any blocked exits or safety hazards to Security

Evacuation Routes

Emergency exits & escape paths

✕
1
Stay Calm
Do not panic. Listen for announcements from campus security or faculty members near you.
2
From Your Location → Evacuation Area
From your specific location, follow the orange evacuation path on the 3D map leading to the nearest emergency exit or assembly area. Tap "Pin on Map" on any exit below to see its route.
3
Arrive at Evacuation Area
Reach the designated evacuation area or assembly point safely. Assist others if needed and do not re-enter any building.
4
Reach the Assembly Area
Proceed to the designated assembly point outside the main gate. Assist anyone who needs help.
5
Report & Await Instructions
Check in with your department head or nearest security officer. Do not re-enter any building until cleared.
